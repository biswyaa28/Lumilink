01 — The Problem

Two sides of the same broken market — and the pain is worth ₹50K+ per transaction.

Funded Startups Waste 6+ Hours

Series-A founders get 70+ bids per post. 90% are AI spam. Vetting eats a full workday. At ₹50K-2L/project, bad hires cost 3x in rework.

Mid-Level Freelancers Get Buried

Talented 2-5yr designers compete against $5/hr bids & AI portfolios. Algorithm favors volume, not quality. Best work never gets seen.

Platforms Extract Without Enabling

20% commissions, connect tokens, paywalled DMs. No contracts, no escrow, no IP protection. Optimized for transactions, not relationships.

Communities Don't Scale

WhatsApp/Discord handle 60%+ of Indian freelance hiring, but lack trust infrastructure: no escrow, vetting, dispute resolution, or contracts.

Frequency × Pain: Hiring is WEEKLY for startups + HIGH pain (₹50K+ at stake) = Top-right quadrant.

10 — AI Risk & Moat

AI kills the bottom, grows the middle.

The Risk

AI tools (Midjourney, Sora, ChatGPT) are replacing commodity creative work. $5 logos, basic landing pages—automatable.

If entry-level freelancers are the target, the TAM is shrinking. This is why we pivoted the wedge from entry-level to mid-level.

Our Thesis & Compounding Moat

We match AI-native creatives: people who use AI as a tool, not compete against it. Skill challenges test AI fluency + creative judgment.
